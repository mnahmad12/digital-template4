Traffic

  • High Concept: Avoid Traffic as you zoom to and interview
  • Player Experience Goals: Reaslinzing how much Route 7 sucks, no matter what you do you will be in traffic, and get late.
  • Genre: Racing?
  • Game play: Use the arrow keys to move arround and avoid the traffic. Each time you git a car the time remaining is decreased.
  • Prototype goal: 1) Movement 2) Collisions
  • Features: Cool INtro screen
  • Setting/Premise: You are late for an interview and have to get there on time. Unfortunately you also have to get on route 7, meanig you have to fight your way through traffic!
  • Story: None
  • Target Audience: Kids/High schoolers who are banned from playing anything actually fun in school
  • Play time: Currently the game is only one level, so around 30 seconds-1 minute.
  • Strategies: Move off the screen, never get hit by traffic!
  • Prototype Assets: All assets were cloned directly from phasers examples: https://github.com/photonstorm/phaser-examples/tree/master/examples/assets/sprites and the internet
